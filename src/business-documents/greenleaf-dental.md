## 8. Cancellation & Late Policy

- Appointments must be canceled at least **24 hours in advance**  
- Late cancellations may result in a **$50 cancellation fee**  
- Patients arriving more than **15 minutes late** may need to reschedule  

---

## 9. Emergency Care

- Emergency dental services are available **during business hours only**  
- After-hours emergency services are **not provided**  
- Patients experiencing severe pain after hours are advised to visit an emergency room  

---

## 10. Accessibility & Clinic Policies

- The clinic is wheelchair accessible  
- Service animals are permitted  
- Face masks are optional  
- Patients under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian  

---

## 11. Services Not Offered

GreenLeaf Dental Clinic does **not** provide:
- Orthodontic services (braces or Invisalign)  
- Dental implants  
- Root canal treatment  
- After-hours emergency services  
- Tele-dentistry appointments  

---

## 12. Important Notes for Patients

- Please arrive **10 minutes early** for your first visit  
- New patients must complete intake forms before treatment  
- Treatment recommendations are made after an initial examination
